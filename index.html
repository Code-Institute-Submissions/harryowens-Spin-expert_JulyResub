Why take a spin class?

Mental wellbeing

Taking a spin class is an immersive experience that let riders switch off from daily stresses and focus on themselves for the duration of the class. This can improve concentraion and overall wellbeing.

Weight loss

A spin class is one of the most intense forms of exercise available, with some riders able to burn up to 1,000 calories in a single one-hour class. This can assist with calorie control as part of a weight loss programme.

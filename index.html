Beginners guide to indoor cycling

Why take a spin class?

Mental wellbeing

Taking a spin class is an immersive experience that let riders switch off from daily stresses and focus on themselves for the duration of the class. This can improve concentraion and overall wellbeing.

Weight loss

A spin class is one of the most intense forms of exercise available, with some riders able to burn up to 1,000 calories in a single one-hour class. This can assist with calorie control as part of a weight loss programme.

Fun

Spinning pushes riders to challenge themselves physically and mentally. Whether embarking on a multi-class programme or just a single ride, learning to get comfortable being uncomfortable is fun and rewarding.

Community

A spin class is naturally a group activity - whether riders are in the same room as each other at a gym or spin studio, or connected online through a platform like Peloton or Soul Cycle, the sense of community is invigorating.
